BoardRecord

Welcome! Here are a few things to get you started:

To start recording, press the camera button on the left.
To stop recording, press the same button again.

Once you stop recording, a segment will be created and displayed as a
color box below. You may select any segment, and play it individually,
or you may play all recordings using the Play All button.

The right sidebar will expand to show various brush options.

You may also edit the speed of the playback by
inputting a number between 1-10.
